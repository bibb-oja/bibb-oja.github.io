## About OJA

OJA analyzes occupational data from job advertisements to make labor market trends visible, support student research, and publish findings for academic and policy audiences.

### What we do

- Analyze skill requirements in job markets
- Identify occupational trends and labor demand shifts
- Apply natural language processing to job posting text
- Connect research to economics and social science perspectives

### Why it matters

Job advertisements are a rich and timely data source for understanding how work is changing, which skills are most valued, and how employers signal labor market needs.
