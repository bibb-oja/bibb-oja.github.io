# Job Advertisement Analysis Research

Welcome to the OJA research initiative. We analyze job advertisements to understand occupational requirements, labor market dynamics, and evolving skills demands.

## Research Focus

Our work explores:
- Skill requirements in job markets
- Occupational trends and dynamics
- Language analysis in job postings
- Labor economics perspectives
